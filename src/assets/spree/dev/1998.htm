A Note From The Coach...

   Welcome to the Spree Programs. The Spree Gymnastics, Color Guard   and Twirling Teams Programs are structured classes uniquely designed to enhance each child's self-esteem, physical ability and pride in a noncompetitive setting. The children progress at their own pace.
    With over 36 years experience working with children ages 2 through 13, and 31 years teaching dance, gymnastics and baton twirling in private schools (1984-2000 in the NW Houston area, and 2001- present in Fort Wayne, Indiana), I look forward to working with your children 
in their safe, enthusiastic and supportive learning environment.  
                                             "Coach Judy"
